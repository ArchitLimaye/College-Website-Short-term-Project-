Welcome to Government Polytechnic, Mumbai

Government Polytechnic Mumbai is an academically autonomous institute of Government of Maharashtra established in 1960, one of the oldest and prestigious institute in Mumbai region and in the state of Maharashtra. Institute got academic autonomy in the year 1994. Institute Had undertaken various Projects like World Bank Assisted Project, Canada India Industry Institute Linkage Project, Technical Education Quality Improvement Program Phase II, etc. Institute has very good liaison with industries. Institute is offering 3 years of AICTE approved diploma sandwich pattern programme in following branches
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Instrumentation Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Leather Technology
  • Leather Goods and Footwear Technology
  • Rubber Technology
